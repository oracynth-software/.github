# Oracynth

A family archive platform with an evidence-first data model and inclusive architecture.

Oracynth helps families organize photos, documents, oral histories, and records into a unified archive built on an open standard. Every fact traces back through citations to sources. Any two people can be connected through researcher-defined relationships, with no assumptions about gender, quantity, or biology.

### Principles

- **Oral histories are evidence.** Audio and transcripts carry the same weight as documents, vital records, or DNA tests.
- **Connections over lineage.** The graph supports chosen family, adoption, godparents, and any relationship a family needs.
- **Assertions over conclusions.** Conflicting evidence is preserved, not silently resolved.
- **Data ownership is structural.** Open format, no export tax.
